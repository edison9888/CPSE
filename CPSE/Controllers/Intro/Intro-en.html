About CPSE

CPSE-China Public Security Expo Founded in 1989 in Shenzhen, CPSE has been successfully organizing 13 sessions. Served more than 6,200 security companies and 281,000 buyers. The largest exhibition in the world and the most influential exhibition in Asia.

The 14th CPSE, a global focus in 2013, brings you the most professional buyers and clients. The Only BPA Certified Security Exhibition in China CPSE, held in Shenzhen, is the only security exhibition in China that has been certified by BPA.
